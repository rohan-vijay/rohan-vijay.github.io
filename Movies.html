Visualisation of Highest-Grossing Films
I have been always fascinated by how few of the movies make a lot of money while on the other hand, a few of the others are not able to do good. I was on my way to find about the top movies with maximum gross amount in the history of films and media. I scraped the data from wikipedia to analyze and visualise the movie titles, along with their total gross amount, year and ranking as per the highest gross amount. The list consist of 50 top movies in the film and media history.

D3.JS based library, raw was used to create the data visualisation. I am still working on a few more visualisations and will keep updated here :)
