TU Delft Serious Game Design Internship

Game Design, User Research, Information Architecture

  1. Portfolio
  2. Tu Delft Serious Game Design Internship

Project Summary: Worked upon a project on serious game, under the guidance of Prof. Natalia Romero, TU Delft Netherlands and Abhigyan Singh, Phd Student at TU Delft Netherlands. During the course of the internship I have been working upon the conceptualisation of a serious game which aims to study hthe energy sharing in a neighbourhood in different scenarios of abundance and scarcity in various energy grids which includes smart grids, mini grids, micro grids, isolated grids, traditional grids and grids of future

A physical prototype of the game was already developed and I was to work upon the digital reconstruction of the game. It started off with research on various grid system and then we worked upon game design principles incuding information architecture, goals, rule book, wireframes, mockups, and paper prototyping of the game. The process involved a thorough literature review of energy sharing system and contextual energy management systems. It also involved critical evaluation of the existing concept via multiple rounds of playtesting using the paper prototypes. The next stage focused on stydying about the game mechanics and conceptualisation of initial version of game. After multiple iteration of wireframes, a solid concept was presented in the form of digital mockups.

Why this game is being developed ?

This is a very important question that needs to be answered because why was this game being developed. There is lot of money being spent to incorporate new technologies to increase the local energy sustainence of a neighbourhood. But it has often been seen that the new technology is sometime not accepted by people and this leads to wastage of millions and billions of dollars. So this game acts as a research tool to study how people share vaious energy grids under various scenarios of enrgy abundance and scarcity.

Future Scope

The project is currently in the development stage and then it shall be taken forward for user testing

Literature Review

A thorough literature review was done in two phases. The first phase included understanding in depth about the various grid systems(mini grid, micro grid, smart grid, and traditional grid). In the second phase, I was to learn about the key concepts of serious games, simulation techniques, gamification, learning theories and game mechanics in details.
