I am working upon a serious game design project, under the guidance of Prof. Natalia Romero. During the course of the internship I have been working upon the conceptualisation of a serious game which aims to study hthe energy sharing in a neighbourhood in different scenarios of abundance and scarcity in various energy grids which includes smart grids, mini grids, micro grids, isolated grids, traditional grids and grids of future

A physical prototype of the game was already developed and I was to work upon the digital reconstruction of the game. It started off with research on various grid system and then we worked upon game design principles incuding information architecture, goals, rule book, wireframes, mockups, and paper prototyping of the game.

Why this game is being developed ?

This is a very important question that needs to be answered because why was this game being developed. There is lot of money being spent to incorporate new technologies to increase the local energy sustainence of a neighbourhood. But it has often been seen that the new technology is sometime not accepted by people and this leads to wastage of millions and billions of dollars. So this game acts as aresearch tool to study how people share vaious energy grids under various scenarios of enrgy abundance and scarcity.
